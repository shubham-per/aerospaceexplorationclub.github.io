WHO ARE WE
Aerospace Exploration Club of pulchowk is a student-led club. Although we are an aerospace-focused club, students of the Pulchowk campus, regardless of major, are welcome and there is no requirement for any prior expertise. Everything you require, including CAD, Openrocket, and knowledge of model rocketry, will be guided and taught to you by our teams.(We aren't experts though)

WHY SHOULD I JOIN?
Why? What are your reasons. Can't wait to hear your opinion. Feel free to reach us through our discord server.

What we do ?
Our Aerospace Club is a club of like-minded individuals who aid each other in their career and lives. Members are able to connect with other students through projects, homework help, and fun club events!
